# Project Scope

## Project Title
E-commerce Funnel Optimization Analysis: Modeling Conversion Drop-offs and Revenue Leakage from Event-Level Data

## Business Problem
E-commerce businesses often lose users between product discovery, cart activity, checkout, and purchase. This project analyzes event-level customer behavior to identify where users drop off, which segments experience the most friction, and where revenue leakage may be occurring.

## Business Question
Where are users dropping off in the e-commerce journey, which segments are causing the most revenue leakage, and what should the business fix first?

## Funnel Stages
The primary funnel will analyze the following customer journey:

1. View Item
2. Add to Cart
3. Begin Checkout
4. Purchase

## Planned Analysis
- Overall funnel conversion
- Step-by-step drop-off rate
- User-level funnel performance
- Session-level funnel performance
- Channel-level conversion
- Device-level conversion
- Product category conversion
- Estimated revenue leakage
- Prioritized business recommendations
